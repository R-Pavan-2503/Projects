SOLAR SYSTEM




The solar system is the collection of eight planets, along with numerous dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets, that orbit around the Sun. It formed approximately 4.6 billion years ago and is located in the Milky Way galaxy. The eight planets, in order from the Sun, are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.
